Channagiri-A Day Trek From Bangalore

Nidesh D Shetty  / 
Distance:
7 km
Difficulty:
Easy
Altitude:
4,478
Base:
Nandi Village

Channagiri, it is towards the north west of the Nandi Hills range, is a less-hiked trail in Chikkaballapura. Locally, it is better known as Channakeshava Betta. It is the source of the North Pinakini River and has an Anjaneya Temple at the top. But there is more to this hill than its famous landmarks. It’s a very great day trek.

  1. Time

    For Beginers,the one way distance can take upto 3 hours.The path to the top is guided by a stone marks which helps to get to the top of the hills.Descending can take upto 2 hours.

  2. Trek Type

    Majorly in the form of stone steps, hidden in dense undergrowth. Small sections of boulders. Gradual ascent all the way to the top.Located about 60 km from Bangalore, this hill is perfect for day and night treks. What makes Channagiri different from other trekking destinations in Karnataka is the fact that it is much cooler thereby offering a pleasanter experience.

  3. Route

    Route starts from the parking point itself,there is a temple on the way to channagiri hills,where you can park your vehicles and start the trek.On the right side of the road there is a way where you have to start your ascending, The route is accomapnied with boulders and greenary plants.Ther are various tunnel type routes surrounded by plants and trees.

    The best thing is route maps are carved on a stone till nagadevara temple.After that you must be careful while ascending you may get lost easily.Follow the foot path dont try to go in a unexplored route. There are various resting points available on the way.For photogenic people this can be the best place.On the top of the hill there are 2 temples and a lake where you can enjoy your time.

    At 1430 metres high, Channagiri offers breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and landscapes. Channagiri along with Nandigiri, Skandagiri, Devagiri and Dibbagiri make up the Nandi range.

    The chilly night temperatures often give way to misty mornings that forms the perfect backdrop to spectacular sunrises. The diverse terrain of Channagiri provides an enjoyable challenge for trekkers – both amateur and experienced. On the way up and among the dense greenery, one can catch a glimpse of the indigenous wildlife too.

  4. Camping

    Camping is allowed,but take the garbage bag and carry your waste with yourself dont spoil the view of the place.

  5. Itenaries

    • Cap
    • Tent
    • Water Bottle
    • Energy Drink
    • Food
    • Fruits
    • Good Shoes
